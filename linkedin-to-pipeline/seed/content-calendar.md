# Content Calendar

The plan `linkedin-calendar-planning` writes and the rest of the loop fills. It holds **open
slots** (stage-shaped, empty) that `idea-mining` and `post-write` fill in. Span = your arc
length (default 90 days); cadence = your posts/week (default 2–3) — both from `brand-profile`.
The agent treats your edits as the source of truth.

## Arc phases

The arc is a **one-time ramp**, not a repeating cycle:

1. **Positioning** — establish who you help and your angle (TOFU-heavy).
2. **Authority** — prove how you think (more MOFU).
3. **Conversion** — invite the conversation (BOFU enters).

## Weekly slots

Each week's slots hold the **70/20/10 rolling-window** mix with spacing rules: TOFU is the
backbone; MOFU ≈ every 5th post; BOFU ≈ every 10th, spaced and always after trust content.

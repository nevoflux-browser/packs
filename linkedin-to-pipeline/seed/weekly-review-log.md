# Weekly Review Log

The feedback loop. `linkedin-weekly-review` appends one entry a week from the numbers you
supply, and keeps the **"What's working"** section current — which `linkedin-post-write` reads
to choose formats and hooks. The agent treats your edits as the source of truth.
